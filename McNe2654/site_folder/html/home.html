ABOUT ME


My name is Amanda McNeil; am nineteen years old and I am an international student from Costa Rica. I started my career in Computer Information Systems: Web Application and Cloud Development at Edmonds College on Fall 2020; that same season I moved to Washington State. Before I came here, I was in a technical High School in which I studied accounting; however, I was able to come to study my real interest, CIS, when I was granted an scolarship that allowed me to study in the US.




WHY TECHNOLOGY?


I always found technology rather interesting, how machines, devices and medicine worked. However, it was not until I was fifteen years old that I started to think that I would like to learn more about it and even persue a career on the field. I started to learn a bit of the basics from tutorials and other information I found on the web; with research, I discovered CIS, which is a career that allowed me to use my creativity in a business/coding based environment.
