My "Aha" moment...


In really enjoyed the class CIS241: Web Development I; I feel it gave the first look into a huge world of opportunities and innovation that I am eager to explore more in the future.

I think my 'Aha'moment was at the halfway point of the class; at the time, I was in the middle of creating this website and for the first time, felt that I kind of new what I was doing, feeling that I finally understood the tags: What function do they have, when to use them and how to style them. I also really enjoyed the process of designing the website and looking for different and engaging ways to deliver the information in every page in a different manner, solidifying my idea that I am really design-visual oriented.

This course also helped me understand the building blocks of development, a 'sneak peak' of what all the possibilities available, and creating this website also helped me to see that this is my first step and I do have the ability to continue!
